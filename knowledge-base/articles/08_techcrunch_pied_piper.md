# Google Unveils TurboQuant — Internet Calls It 'Pied Piper'

> **Fonte**: https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/25/google-turboquant-ai-memory-compression-silicon-valley-pied-piper/
> **Autor**: Sarah Perez
> **Plataforma**: TechCrunch
> **Data**: March 25, 2026

## Summary

Google introduced TurboQuant, a new AI memory compression algorithm that promises to reduce AI's "working memory" by up to 6x.

The algorithm's capabilities sparked comparisons to the fictional "Pied Piper" compression technology from HBO's *Silicon Valley*, where a startup's revolutionary data compression technology becomes central to their business model.

## Key Details

- TurboQuant remains in the laboratory research phase
- Not yet deployed for practical applications
- The technology is still experimental
- Generated massive social media discussions
